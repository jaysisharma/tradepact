Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 02, 2026

Executive Summary

At TradePact, we prioritize the privacy and security of your trading data. We collect only what is necessary to provide you with high-quality journaling and AI-driven insights. Your data is stored securely via Firebase and is never sold to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a professional trading experience, we collect the following categories of data:

  • Account Data: Email address provided during registration (managed via Firebase Authentication).
  • Trading Data: Trade logs, entry/exit prices, symbols, performance metrics, and journal entries.
  • Media Data: Chart screenshots you upload for AI analysis.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized technical information such as device type and OS version.

2. Use of Information

We use your data to power the core features of TradePact:

  • Personalized trade tracking and performance analytics.
  • AI-driven behavioral coaching via Google Gemini.
  • Technical support and service improvements.

3. Advertising & Analytics

We may use third-party service providers to show advertisements to You to help support and maintain Our Service. These third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob) may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your activities on this and other websites and apps to provide you with targeted advertising based on your interests.

4. AI Intelligence & Privacy

TradePact utilizes Google Gemini AI to analyze screenshots of your trading charts. This processing is strictly technical and automated. We do not use your personal trade data to train public AI models, and all interactions with AI services are conducted through secure API channels.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You have full control over your information:

  • Manual Deletion: You can delete specific trades or journal entries at any time.
  • Account Deletion: You may permanently delete your account and all associated data through the application's settings menu.

6. Security Standards

We employ industry-standard security measures, including end-to-end encryption for data in transit and secure cloud storage via Firebase, to ensure your financial setups remain confidential.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact our privacy team at:

privacy@tradepact.com
