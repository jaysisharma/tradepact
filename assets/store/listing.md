## Full Description (Play Store — max 4000 chars)

**Make a pact. Keep it.**

TradePact is the only trading journal designed specifically for prop firm traders — FTMO, Funding Pips, The5ers, and beyond. Stop losing funded accounts to bad habits. Start tracking what actually matters.

---

### 📊 Trade Journal
Log every trade in seconds. Capture pair, direction, entry, SL, TP, and exit price. TradePact auto-calculates P&L and risk-reward ratio so you never have to do the math.

Supported instruments: XAUUSD, EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDJPY, and any custom pair.

---

### 🧠 Discipline Score
Your prop firm doesn't just care about profits — they care about *how* you trade. TradePact scores every trade on three pillars:

- **Followed your plan** — 40 points
- **No revenge or impulse mood** — 30 points
- **Respected your stop loss** — 30 points

See your average discipline score across all trades. Elite traders score 75+. Where do you stand?

---

### 🔥 Day Streak
Build a streak of consecutive trading days. Miss a day and it resets to zero. Consistency is the foundation of funded account success — TradePact gamifies it.

---

### 😤 Mood Analytics *(Pro)*
Are you profitable when you're confident? Do you revenge-trade and blow the session? TradePact shows your win rate broken down by mood — Confident, Neutral, Anxious, Bored, Revenge. Face the data. Fix the habit.

---

### 📸 Screenshot Auto-Parse *(Pro)*
Take a photo of your chart setup. TradePact uses Google Gemini AI to automatically extract the pair, direction, entry, SL, and TP — filling your trade form in one tap.

---

### 🤖 Weekly AI Insights *(Pro)*
Every Sunday, TradePact's AI analyzes your last 7 days of trades and delivers 3 sharp, personalised bullet points: your top pattern, your biggest weakness, and one concrete improvement for next week.

---

### 🏢 Prop Firm Mode
Configure your firm's rules once:

- Daily loss limit (e.g. $500 for a $10K account)
- Maximum drawdown (e.g. $1,000)

TradePact shows real-time progress bars on your dashboard. When you're within 20% of your daily limit, you get a clear warning — before you blow the account.

---

### 🌙 Dark-Only Design
Designed for trading terminals. No light mode. No distractions. Gold on black, built for focus.

---

### 🔒 Your Data, Secure
Trades are stored in Firebase — backed up, synced across devices, and accessible only by you.
